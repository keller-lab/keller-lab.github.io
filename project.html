Example Projects

MACH: Megalopolitan Coastal Transformation Hub

Researching complex interactions between climate hazards and communities to inform governance of coastal risk.

PCHES: Program for Coupled Human and Earth Systems

Advancing the quantitative understanding of multisector dynamics.

HEALED: Health Effects of Deep Decarbonization

Analyzing potential synergies between deep decarbonization of the energy system and reducing (inequities in) negative health impacts.

ICOM: Integrated Coastal Modeling

Developing, evaluating, and applying a diverse set of modeling tools to systematically analyze coastal processes, hazards, and responses.
